Mama is the kind of mom who cheers the loudest during your sports tournament, or the one who fights wars for you when she sees you hurt, or the one who stays up late at night with you to help you with your project. Understanding

First of all, I honor you po for being a mom who sets family as your number one priority despite the busy schedule you have. Thank you for helping with all our problems be it acamedic related or not kahit na ikaw mismo maraming quotations or work na ginagawa. Second, for being the emotional backbone in our family especially at times na nagkaka misunderstanding sa atin. I honor you for always choosing to see the good in people even if sometimes it comes to a point na umaabot na ng extreme measures. Third, for pushing us to achieve our dreams and become a better person. Thank you mama for always pushing us to aim high and for encouraging us whenever we fail. Lastly, for the unconditional love you continue to shower us with. I am truly blessed to have a mother like you, and I’m pretty sure any kid would think so too. Sobrang lucky namin na may patuloy na nagmamahal at nagaaruga saming magkakapatid and I thank God for you everyday. Happy Mother’s day mama! Love you!

Love, Manta

Steadfast

In spite of it all, we honor you for unceasingly understanding and loving us all. You bear the source of joy in our home and you will always be in our hearts forever. We love you, really.
